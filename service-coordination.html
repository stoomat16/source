What is Construction Coordination?


Coordination is the art of harmonizing the many moving parts of a construction project, from design and planning to execution and completion. It involves meticulous planning, communication, and collaboration among all project stakeholders to avoid conflicts, delays, and cost overruns. With the right coordination, your project can achieve optimal results and meet or exceed your goals.

What is Construction Administration?

Construction Administration is the crucial phase that bridges the gap between project design and actual construction. It involves project coordination, quality control, budget management, and on-site supervision to bring the design vision to life. This phase is where the rubber meets the road, and the success of your project depends on efficient and knowledgeable administration.

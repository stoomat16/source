What is Laser Scanning?

Laser scanning is a state-of-the-art technology that captures precise 3D representations of physical objects, structures, and spaces using laser beams. It allows us to create detailed point cloud data that can be transformed into highly accurate 3D models. Laser scanning revolutionizes surveying, documentation, and project planning by providing a comprehensive digital record of real-world conditions.

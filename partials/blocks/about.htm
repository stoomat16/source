About Us

Pioneering BIM Excellence Since 2000

At BIM Technologies, we are trailblazers in the field of Building Information Modeling (BIM). Established in 2000, our journey began with a vision to streamline construction processes and elevate project outcomes.

Our firm specializes in supporting contractors and owners throughout the design-construction-turnover process by providing BIM services at every level. We are MEP and Process Piping Specialist from Design to Fabrication and Install and have over 25 years of experience in these complex systems.
